About Me

As mentioned in the homepage, I started programming at 10 years old. However, I haven’t been consistent with it because I often get interested in so many things at once.

I used to be a workaholic and would work on a lot of personal projects when I was younger. Right when school was done, I remember that I would immediately work on projects I've set up for myself.

Nowadays, I’ve calmed down. I still like getting things done and I usually create tools or plugins to help me with that.

Career Goals

I don’t have any grandiose goals just as my father wished for when he got me into programming, and I doubt that my work will turn me into a millionaire.

I however, like making tools or plugins that I will use in my day to day life. I often find very specific things that I need since there aren’t any other software or plugins that does what I specifically need.

Back when I was young, I often programmed for fun. I often made games but it never had a practical use in my life for it.

It was only within this year that I've gotten to use my programming skills for something practical and useful in my life, and I enjoy using things I've made, especially if it’s useful.
